About Us

The Pan American School Model United Nations (PASMUN) is an elementary and middle school level conference in San Pedro Garza García, Nuevo León. It was first founded in 2013 as an internal, once-a-year conference at PAS, as a way for our students to showcase the skills they have acquired throughout the year and to bring the school year to a close. In 2015, PASMUN opened itself to outside schools for the first time. Since then, it has grown from around a hundred participants to more than 250 participants, with students coming across from different parts of the surounding area.
